About Me

I completed my MSc in physics at University of Ottawa under the supervision of Prof. Richard Naud. My research focused on computational neuroscience, specifically the transient amplification network response from intracortical microstimulations. https://doi.org/10.20381/ruor-31496
I have an Honours Bachelor's of Science degree from University of Toronto, where I completed a double major in Physics and Statistics, with a minor in Mathematics.
I come from the island of Montreal, Canada. My hobbies include puzzles and physical health. I have an interest in data science, finance, and neuroscience, and am aspiring to a career in either neurotechnology or quantitative finance.
